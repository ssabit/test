LazyChat

Description

  • Saad Ibna Omar Sabit

  • Lazychat

LazyChat is a social commerce SaaS tool that offers an omnichannel ChatCommerce bot for online businesses. It helps businesses sell products on different messaging channels, such as Messenger, Instagram, Viber, and WhatsApp while managing their catalogs and orders from a single platform. LazyChat's e-commerce tools help businesses automate their sales, improve customer experience and engagement, and bring repeat customers. Its goal is to simplify social commerce and become the leading SaaS in the social commerce and e-commerce industry worldwide.
